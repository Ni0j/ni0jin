Nio Jin:

is  working at the intersection of creative technology and systemic storytelling.+

Their early interest in law stemmed from a fascination with evidence and argumentation, while physics offered a way to think about the underlying structures of reality.

Treating design as a living logic system, their work explores how perception, belief, and meaning are constructed, negotiated, and sometimes manipulated.

Drawing from interests in religion, divination, and music, they translate abstract systems into tangible forms and experiences.
